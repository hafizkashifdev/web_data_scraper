Open Banking Developer Portal

We would like to invite Developers / Third Party Providers (TPPs) to work with our Account Information & Payment Initiation APIs as per OBIE standards. This portal allows self service for on-boarding and also support SCA compliant customer authentication. Most of all it supports fully testable AISP & PISP flows.

Accounts

AISPs are firms which provide an online service to its customers allowing them to access their payment account information held by a given current account provider, typically a credit institution.

Payments

Banks should provide APIs so that any Payment Initiation Service Provider (PISP) can initiate a credit transfer. PISPs will be regulated and known by all other banks.

CBPII

A CoF request is made by the CBPII to confirm with the ASPSP that the necessary funds are currently available in a PSU's bank account.

Inside our flexible APIs

Accounts API

API Accounts makes it possible to share information from demand deposit accounts, savings accounts and prepaid payment accounts such as limits, transactions and balances.

Payments API

The API aims to collect consent and carry out the initiation of payment between banks and financial institutions and is also accessible to merchants participating in Open Banking.

CBPII API

A CoF request is made by the CBPII to confirm with the ASPSP that the necessary funds are currently available in a PSU's bank account.
